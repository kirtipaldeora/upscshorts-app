Privacy Policy — Penni

Effective 7 July 2026

Penni is a UPSC exam-preparation app offering daily current-affairs briefings, previous-year questions, and a geography quiz arcade.

Data we collect

None. Penni has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no ads, and no tracking. We do not collect, transmit, store, sell, or share any personal information.

Data stored on your device

Your preferences and activity — bookmarks, theme choice, selected GS papers, imported content, and quiz progress — are stored only in your device's local storage. They never leave your device. Uninstalling the app (or clearing its site data) permanently deletes them.

Network use

The app downloads its study content (articles, questions, and map data) from our own hosting and public map-data repositories. These requests do not include any personal information. If you choose to use the optional AI answer-evaluation feature, the photos of the answer you upload and your own API key are sent directly from your device to Anthropic's Claude API to generate the evaluation; we never see or store them on any server. Your API key and evaluations are stored only on your device. Content is cached on your device so the app also works offline.

Permissions

Penni requests no special device permissions. The optional "Import JSON" feature reads only the file you explicitly select.

Children

Penni does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children, because it collects no data at all.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new effective date.
