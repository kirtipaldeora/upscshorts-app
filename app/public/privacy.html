Privacy Policy

Effective 15 July 2026

Penni is a learning and revision app for UPSC aspirants. This policy explains what information the app uses and the choices available to you.

Information you provide

If you create an account, Penni may store your name, contact details, profile photo or icon, target exam, preparation preferences and optional profile information. Practice answers, bookmarks, progress, streaks and Mains-evaluation history are used to provide the learning experience.

Local and cloud storage

Guest-mode activity is stored on your device. Signed-in accounts may sync profile and learning state through Supabase so progress is available across devices. A personal AI API key, when supplied in Advanced Tools, is stored locally on that device and is not included in Penni cloud sync.

Notifications and device features

Penni asks for notification permission only when you enable revision reminders. Email updates and WhatsApp briefings are separate, optional choices. Penni sends them only after you opt in during setup or in Settings, and you can turn either channel off at any time. Haptic feedback and motion effects can be disabled in Settings.

Service providers

Penni may use Supabase for authentication and storage, OAuth providers for sign-in, Resend for opted-in product email, Meta WhatsApp Business for opted-in WhatsApp messages, and an AI service when you explicitly request answer evaluation. Those services process the information needed to provide their service under their own privacy terms.

Deletion and retention

You can clear device data or permanently delete a signed-in Penni account from Settings. Account deletion removes the associated synced profile and learning state. Some operational records may be retained where required for security or legal compliance.

Your choices

  • Use guest mode without cloud sync.
  • Disable email updates, WhatsApp briefings, notifications, haptics or animated backgrounds.
  • Edit optional profile fields at any time.
  • Export or remove locally stored content from Settings.

Contact

Questions or privacy requests can be sent to support@penni.app.
